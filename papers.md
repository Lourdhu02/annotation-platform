# Papers on Annotation Quality, Active Learning & IAA Metrics

1. **Counting on Consensus: Selecting the Right Inter-annotator
   Agreement Metric for NLP Annotation and Evaluation** (2026)
   - arxiv.org/abs/2603.06865
   - Reviews Cohen's κ, Fleiss' κ, Krippendorff's α; covers
   label imbalance, missing data, confidence intervals.

2. **Position: Early-Stage Quality Assurance in Annotation
   Pipelines Is More Cost-Effective Than Late-Stage Validation**
   (2026) - arxiv.org/abs/2605.15714
   - Proposes T0/T1/T2 QA trigger taxonomy; shift-left principle
   for annotation pipelines.

3. **The Annotation Pipeline Is Production Infrastructure** (2026)
   - tianpan.co/blog/2026-04-15-annotation-pipeline-production-infrastructure
   - Argues annotation as software engineering; queues, schemas,
   monitoring, versioned dataset stores.

4. **Data Annotation Quality Control: A 2026 Field Guide**
   - dataxpower.com/blog/data-annotation-quality-control
   - Seven operational artefacts: gold panels, disagreement-
   cluster reports, multi-pass review, per-class IAA.

5. **Counting on Consensus (IAA Metric Selection)** – arxiv 2026
   - Organises IAA measures by task type; best practices for
   transparent reporting of disagreement patterns.
